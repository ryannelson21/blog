Product thesis

The blog is a readable essay first, and a transparent edit trail second. Readers can opt into provenance without losing the flow of the piece.

Core experience

Clean typography with no annotations. This is the default.

Feature roadmap

MVP (2–3 weeks)

  • Reader ↔ Provenance toggle.
  • Origin color-coding with draft badges.
  • Hover tooltips with provenance metadata.
  • Bottom-of-post edit log.

V1 (6–8 weeks)

  • Timeline scrubber for draft playback.
  • Inline diffs for sentence changes.
  • Exportable receipt for sharing.
  • Public JSON for provenance data.
